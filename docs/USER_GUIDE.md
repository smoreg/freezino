## ğŸ° Casino Games

### 1. Roulette

**How to Play**:
1. Select your chip value (bet amount)
2. Place chips on the betting board:
   - **Number Bet**: Bet on a specific number (0-36)
   - **Red/Black**: Bet on color
   - **Odd/Even**: Bet on odd or even numbers
   - **Dozens**: Bet on groups (1-12, 13-24, 25-36)
3. Click **"Spin"**
4. Watch the wheel spin and see if you win!

**Payouts**:
- Number (straight): 35:1
- Red/Black: 1:1
- Odd/Even: 1:1
- Dozen: 2:1

**House Edge**: ~2.7% (European roulette with single zero)

### 2. Slots

**How to Play**:
1. Choose your bet amount
2. Click **"Spin"** to start
3. Watch the 5 reels spin
4. Win by matching symbols on paylines

**Symbols** (lowest to highest):
- ğŸ’ Cherry

**Winning Combinations**:
- 3 of a kind: Small payout

- 5 of a kind: Large payout
- 5 Sevens: JACKPOT!

### 3. Blackjack (Live)

**How to Play**:
1. Place your bet
2. Receive 2 cards (dealer gets 2 as well)
3. Choose your action:
   - **Hit**: Get another card
   - **Stand**: Keep your current hand
   - **Double**: Double your bet and get 1 card
   - **Split**: Split pairs into two hands (if you have matching cards)
4. Try to get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over

**Card Values**:
- Number cards: Face value
- Face cards (J, Q, K): 10
- Ace: 1 or 11 (whichever is better)

**Special Hands**:
- **Blackjack**: Ace + 10-value card (pays 3:2)
- **Bust**: Over 21 (you lose)

### 4. Crash

**How to Play**:
1. Place your bet
2. Set your cashout multiplier (e.g., 2.0x)
3. Watch the multiplier increase from 1.00x
4. The game "crashes" at a random point
5. If you cash out before the crash, you win!

**Strategy**: Higher multipliers = higher risk, but bigger rewards

### 5. Hi-Lo

**How to Play**:
1. Place your bet
2. See the current number
3. Predict if the next number will be **Higher** or **Lower**
4. Win if your prediction is correct!

**Payout**: 1.9:1 (slightly less than 2:1 due to house edge)

### 6. Wheel

**How to Play**:
1. Place your bet
2. Click **"Spin the Wheel"**
3. The wheel stops on a multiplier
4. Your bet is multiplied by that amount

**Multipliers**: 0x, 1.2x, 1.5x, 2x, 3x, 5x, 10x, 50x
