# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 8, 2024**

## 1. Introduction

At **Freezino**, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, how we store it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

Freezino is an educational platform designed to combat gambling addiction. We collect only the minimum data necessary to provide our service.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Authentication Data (via Google OAuth)
When you sign in to Freezino using Google OAuth, we collect:
- **Email address** (from your Google account)
- **Full name** (from your Google account)
- **Google User ID** (unique identifier)
- **Profile picture URL** (optional, if provided by Google)

### 2.2 Usage Data
We automatically collect the following data during your use of the service:
- **Virtual balance and transactions** (in-game currency only)
- **Game statistics** (games played, bets placed, wins/losses)
- **Work session data** (time worked, virtual earnings)
- **Purchased items** (virtual property in the game)
- **Account creation and last login timestamps**

### 2.3 Technical Data
We may collect:
- **Browser type and version**
- **Device type** (desktop, mobile, tablet)
- **IP address** (for security purposes only)
- **Session data** (for maintaining your login)

### 2.4 Data We DO NOT Collect
We **do not** collect:
- Payment information (no real money is involved)
- Precise geolocation data
- Social Security numbers or government IDs
- Personal financial information
- Health information

## 3. How We Use Your Data

### 3.1 Service Provision
We use your data to:
- **Authenticate your identity** (via Google OAuth)
- **Maintain your account** and virtual balance
- **Track your game statistics** and progress
- **Provide educational insights** about gambling behavior
- **Enable service features** (work timer, shop, games)

### 3.2 Educational Purpose
We use aggregated, anonymized statistics to:
- Demonstrate the financial impact of gambling
- Compare virtual earnings with real-world salaries
- Show patterns of gambling behavior

### 3.3 Security and Fraud Prevention
We use your data to:
- Protect against unauthorized access
- Prevent abuse of the service
- Verify age requirements (18+)

### 3.4 Communication
We may use your email address to:
- Send important service updates
- Respond to your support requests
- Notify you of Terms of Service changes

**Note:** We do not send marketing emails or promotional content.

## 4. How We Store Your Data

### 4.1 Database
Your data is stored in a **SQLite database** on our server. This includes:
- User account information
- Game statistics and transactions
- Virtual items and balance

### 4.2 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encrypted connections** (HTTPS/TLS)
- **Secure authentication** (JWT tokens)
- **Password protection** (delegated to Google OAuth)
- **Regular security updates**

### 4.3 Data Location
Your data is stored on servers located in [Server Location]. We do not transfer your data internationally without appropriate safeguards.

### 4.4 Data Retention
We retain your data:
- **While your account is active** (for service provision)
- **90 days after account deletion** (for fraud prevention)
- **Aggregated statistics** (anonymized, indefinitely for research)

## 5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

### 5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
**Freezino does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.**

### 5.2 Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:

**Google OAuth** (for authentication)
- **Purpose:** User authentication
- **Data shared:** Email, name, Google User ID
- **Privacy Policy:** [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

**Note:** We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking pixels.

### 5.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your data if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal processes (court orders, subpoenas)
- Protect our rights and property
- Prevent fraud or illegal activity
- Protect user safety

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

Under the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)** and other privacy laws, you have the following rights:

### 6.1 Right to Access
You have the right to request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

**How to request:** Email us at privacy@freezino.com

### 6.2 Right to Rectification
You have the right to correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

**How to request:** Update your profile in account settings or contact us.

### 6.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")
You have the right to request deletion of your personal data.

**How to request:** Email us at privacy@freezino.com with subject "Delete My Account"

**Note:** We may retain anonymized statistics for research purposes.

### 6.4 Right to Data Portability
You have the right to receive your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.

**How to request:** Email us at privacy@freezino.com with subject "Data Export"

### 6.5 Right to Restrict Processing
You have the right to request that we limit how we use your data.

### 6.6 Right to Object
You have the right to object to certain types of data processing.

### 6.7 Right to Withdraw Consent
You can withdraw your consent to data processing at any time by deleting your account.

## 7. Cookies and Local Storage

### 7.1 Cookies We Use
We use the following cookies:

**Essential Cookies:**
- **Authentication token** (JWT) – Required for login
- **Language preference** – Saves your language choice
- **Cookie consent choice** – Remembers your cookie preferences

**Functional Cookies:**
- **UI preferences** – Dark mode, sound settings, etc.

### 7.2 No Tracking Cookies
We **do not** use:
- Advertising cookies
- Third-party tracking cookies
- Analytics cookies (unless you opt-in)

For more information, see our [Cookie Policy](/cookies).

## 8. Children's Privacy

Freezino is **not intended for individuals under 18 years of age**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we discover that a user is under 18, we will:
- Delete their account immediately
- Remove all associated data
- Block further access

If you believe a child under 18 has created an account, please contact us at privacy@freezino.com.
